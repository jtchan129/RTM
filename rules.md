**General Overview**
Real Time Mafia is like the classic party game Mafia except that **(a)** public executions, killings,
and special actions are performed on a **daily** basis, and **(b)** there are a bunch of special roles
to make things interesting. The players are divided into three **factions** : the **Villagers** (who are
trying to publicly execute the mafia and the lethal neutrals), the **Mafia** (who are trying to kill
enough villagers so that they are the majority), and the **Neutrals** (who are each trying to fulfill
their own ulterior motive). People die in one of three ways:
● Being executed during the day by a majority vote of all the players
● Being assassinated at night by the **Mafia** , at the command of the Godfather
● Being killed at night by a player with special actions
Each morning, the moderators will send out the results of who died. Players discuss the game
throughout the day, and **Mafia** discusses amongst themselves who to assassinate.
**Team Setup**
● 1/4 of the players are **Mafia** (rounded up) (assign first)
● 1/7 of the players are **Neutrals** (rounded down) (assign second)
● The rest of the players are **Villagers** (assign third)
● Roles are distributed randomly based on category based assignments as follows
○ Town Investigative: Detective, Cop, Tracker, Watcher
○ Town Killing: Vigilante, Bodyguard, Veteran, Bomb
○ Town Support: Mayor, Bus Driver, Escort, Doctor
○ Town Random: Any of the previous Town roles
○ Mafia: Lookout, Framer, Sniper, Yakuza, Janitor, Limo Driver, Hooker, Stalker,
Sniper, Saboteur
○ Neutral: Amnesiac, Arsonist, Jester, Witch, Serial Killer, Survivor, Mass Murderer
○ Specific roles can be specified (for example, Godfather will need to be specified
because there is always one in every game)
○ Specific roles can also be constrained to limit how many are generated in each
game (for example, Godfather will need to be specified because there cannot be
more than one in every game)
**How to Play
● At the beginning of the game:** You will be sent an email with your role, along with a
description of what it can do. If you are **Mafia** , you will be notified of the other Mafia
members (unless you are the Saboteur).
● **Every day before 6:00 PM:** Vote for who to execute on the Google Sheet (link will be
sent out every day in an email).
○ The person with the most votes will be executed and removed from the game.


○ “No Vote” is a valid vote. If “No Vote” is the most voted upon, no public execution
will occur.
○ If no vote is cast, then it will be an abstention, not a no execution.
■ An abstention is very much different from a “no vote” because it changes
the votes required to execute someone
**● Every day before 10:00 PM:** Send in your action by filling out the Night Actions Form
○ On Sundays, if the house meeting goes past 9:30 PM, night actions can be sent
in up to an hour after the end of the meeting.
**● Every day before 12:00 PM (noon):** The results of the previous night will be sent out.
The roles of players that died will be revealed (unless they were disposed by the
Janitor)
● **Whenever you want to, if you are still alive:** Talk about the game!
○ Talk to people about juj who you want to execute.
○ Discuss what roles you think other people are.
○ If you want to, claim to be a role (whether it be true or false).
○ You can talk over any mode of communication, but...
**● NEVER DO ANY OF THE FOLLOWING:**
○ When dead, talk about the game to living players
○ During the game, show anyone an email you SENT the moderator.
○ During the game, show anyone an email you RECEIVED from the moderator.
○ Change someone else’s vote on the Google Sheet
○ Reply All to the night action request
**○ Talk about “executing” people on Campus/any public place
○ Failure to follow these rules may result in disqualification
● Wills:** You may send the moderators (jtchan@wpi.edu) an email representing your
“Will”. If you are killed this email will be sent out to the remaining players. If you are
publicly executed, it will be sent out that evening. If you are killed at night, it will be sent
in the morning. This Will must be sent BEFORE your death (i.e. before 6:30 PM if you’re
executed and before 10:00 PM if you’re killed at night.) If you send multiple Wills, the
most recent one will be used.
**● When one of the endgame conditions is satisfied:** The results will be sent out by
email, along with something special, probably.
**Game Endings and Win Conditions**
● **Mafia** and **lethal Neutrals** are all dead **-> Villagers** win, game ends
○ Lethal **Neutrals** include Mass Murderer, Serial Killer, and Arsonist
**● Mafia** outnumber **Villagers** , and **lethal Neutrals** are dead **-> Mafia** wins, game ends
● **Neutrals** each have special win conditions. Sometimes they can win alongside the
other two factions, and sometimes multiple neutrals can win. Some neutral wins are


game-ending; others allow the game to continue. See the Neutral Roles section for
details.
● Three consecutive days pass without any player dying **->**
○ Execution voting changes to a secret ballot.
○ Play continues.
● Four consecutive days pass without any player dying **->**
○ More **Villagers** than **Mafia** remain alive **-> Villagers** win
○ Otherwise **-> Mafia** win
● Note: Even if you die, you can still win! For example, if you are a **Villager** and you die,
but the remaining **Villagers** manage to win the game, you are considered a winner.
● Note: Special cases may arise in which the moderators will use their discretion and
common sense to declare a certain player or set of players as the winner. For example,
if there is an Escort and a Serial Killer left, and the Escort is continuously seducing the
Serial Killer, the Serial Killer will be declared the winner.
**Village Roles
● Mayor**
○ Can reveal himself at any time at discretion of Moderators
○ Once revealed, vote counts as 3 votes (even if they are later converted to a
different role)
○ Unique role: can only be one Mayor
**● Cop**
○ Can target one player per night to investigate
○ Investigated player appears innocent ( **Villager** , **Neutral** , Godfather) or guilty
(Vigilante, **Mafia** )
**● Doctor**
○ Can target one player per night to protect
○ Protected player can not be killed (equivalent to go being immune to death at
night) or converted to **Mafia** at night (except for in special cases, which are
described in other roles)
○ Will be told they protected their target if their target is attacked and survived
regardless of previous night immunity
○ Can protect self once per game by targeting self with ability (Witch and Bus/Limo
Driver can cause you to target yourself and use this action)
**● Bus Driver**
○ Can target two players per night to switch (player A and player B)
○ Actions that would occur to player A occur to player B
○ Actions that would occur to player B occur to player A
○ Immune to seduction and control
○ Unique role: can only be one Bus Driver
**● Tracker**


○ Can target one player per night to follow
○ If followed player performs an action, the Tracker learns the followed player’s first
target
**● Watcher**
○ Can target one player per night to watch
○ Learns every person that targeted the watched player
**● Escort**
○ Can target one player per night to seduce
○ If seduced player tries to perform an action, the action does not occur
○ Immune to seduction
**● Vigilante**
○ Can target one person at night to slay
○ Slain player dies
○ Action can be used up to 3 times throughout game
○ Appears guilty to Cop
**● Veteran**
○ Can go on guard at night
○ While on guard, anyone that targets them will die regardless of immunity
○ Action can be used up to 3 times throughout game
○ Non-targeting ability, meaning this ability counts as either being used or not and
does not show up when being tracked or watched as well as the action being
unaffected by switch
○ Immune to seduction and control
**● Bomb**
○ If killed by any means at night, explodes and all attackers also die regardless of
immunity
○ At most one Bomb per game
**● Bodyguard**
○ Can target one player (besides yourself) per night to defend
○ If defended player is attacked, both the Bodyguard and all attackers die
(regardless of immunity) **instead** of the defended player
● **Detective**
○ Can target one player per night to scrutinize
○ Will be informed if scrutinized player has a lethal action or not
■ Roles with lethal actions are Vigilante, Veteran, Bomb, Bodyguard, Serial
Killer, Mass Murderer, Arsonist, Godfather, Saboteur, and Sniper
**Mafia Roles
● Mafioso**


○ Basic **Mafia** member
○ Has no actions
○ Will not be assigned at the beginning of the game. Only created if someone is
corrupted by the Yakuza
**● Godfather**
○ Exactly one Godfather in every game
○ Can target one player to assassinate per night
○ Assassinated player dies
○ Appears as innocent for Cop
○ **Mafia** members vote on new Godfather (can only elect current Mafia members)
■ If there is no Godfather after a night (meaning the previous Godfather died
last execution or last night), Mafia members must elect a new Godfather
BEFORE the next execution (6 pm of that day).
**● Limo Driver
○** ( **Mafia** version of Bus Driver)
○ Chooses two targets at night (player A and player B)
○ Actions that would occur to player A occur to player B
○ Actions that would occur to player B occur to player A
○ Immune to seduction and control
○ Unique role: can only be one Limo Driver
**● Stalker**
○ ( **Mafia** version of Tracker)
○ Can target one player per night to follow
○ If followed player performs an action, the Stalker learns the followed player’s first
target
**● Lookout
○** ( **Mafia** version of Watcher)
○ Can target one player per night to watch
○ Learns every person that targeted the watched player
**● Hooker
○** ( **Mafia** version of Escort)
○ Can target one player per night to seduce
○ If seduced player tries to perform an action, the action does not occur
○ Immune to seduction
**● Janitor**
○ Can target one player per night to dispose
○ If the disposed player dies that night, the Janitor and ONLY the Janitor will be
informed of their role.
■ Once this occurs, Janitor loses their ability to dispose players for the
remainder of the game
■ The disposed player’s Will will not be released


**● Framer**
○ Can only exist if there is a Cop
○ Can target one player per night to frame
○ Framed player appears guilty to Cop that night
**● Yakuza**
○ Can target one player per night to corrupt
○ Corruption fails if the target is immune at night or they were protected by doctor
or bodyguard
○ Corruption fails if the target would die the same night
○ Upon use, Yakuza dies and the target is converted to a Mafioso
**■ Mafia** is notified of their new member
○ If corruption is successful and Yakuza dies, Yakuza’s role is not revealed (they
will be revealed as a different **Mafia** role besides Godfather or Mafioso).
○ If corruption is successful the same night the Yakuza is attacked and killed, the
attacker will get the message they killed the Yakuza but the corruption will still
happen and they will appear as a random **Mafia** role
○ If Yakuza targets a mafia the corruption fails
○ Cannot be saved by doctor when using corruption
**● Saboteur**
○ Can target one player per night to sabotage
○ If Saboteur is publicly executed the next day, that sabotaged player will die
regardless of immunity.
○ Is not revealed to **Mafia** at beginning of game
○ Is not informed of who **Mafia** are at beginning of game
○ Cannot become Godfather unless the only **Mafia** member left
○ Unique role: can only be one Saboteur
**● Sniper**
○ Can target one player to snipe at night. Can do this once per game
○ Sniped target dies regardless of immunity
○ Unique role: can only be one Sniper
**Neutral Roles
● Jester**
○ If publicly executed, one player who voted to execute him will be randomly killed
the next night regardless of night immunity
○ Wins (non-game-ending) if executed; loses otherwise.
**● Serial Killer**
○ Can target one player at night to slaughter
○ Slaughtered player dies
○ Immune to death at night


○ Wins (game-ending) if still alive when all **Villagers** and **Mafia** are dead; loses
otherwise.
**● Mass Murderer
○** Can choose one target at night to ambush
○ Any players that target ambushed player die
○ Additionally, if ambushed player does not target anyone, he also dies
○ Can ambush self, in which case Mass Murderer does not die but players that
target him do
○ Cannot use ambush two nights in a row
○ Getting seduced counts as not using ambush
○ Wins (game-ending) if still alive when all **Villagers** and **Mafia** are dead; loses
otherwise.
○ Immune to death at night
**● Arsonist**
○ Each night, can choose to either:
■ Douse a player
■ Un-douse a player
■ Ignite, which kills all doused players regardless of immunity (Including
targets doused by another Arsonist)
○ Wins (game-ending) if still alive when all **Villagers** and **Mafia** are dead; loses
otherwise.
○ Ignite is a non-targeting ability, meaning this ability counts as either being used or
not and does not show up when being tracked or watched as well as the action
being unaffected by switch
○ If ignite is selected, control will not change the effect but the Witch will still get a
message they controlled the Arsonist.
○ Immune to death at night
**● Witch**
○ Each night, can choose two targets (player A and player B)
○ Forces player A to target player B
○ Wins (game-ending) if still alive when all **Villagers** and **Mafia** are dead; loses
otherwise.
○ Immune to seduction and control
**● Amnesiac**
○ At night, can permanently take on the role of any dead player except Godfather
○ Cannot win the game as Amnesiac role
■ Faction, abilities, and win conditions are transferred
● They gain the ability as it would be at game start even if that role
has already used an action. (They would get 3 Vigilante bullets no
matter how many shots Vigilante has used)
**■ Mafia** are notified if a **Mafia** role is taken (except for hidden **Mafia** roles)


■ Everyone is notified “an amnesiac has remembered they are....”
■ Targeting a Yakuza who was revealed as a different role will still have the
Amnesiac become a Yakuza (And alert the town that a dead mafia
member was a Yakuza)
○ Cannot be seen by tracking roles
○ Unique role: can only be one Amnesiac
**● Survivor**
○ At night, can use bulletproof vest on themselves a maximum of 4 times per game
○ Using bulletproof vest makes survivor immune at night for that night
○ Wins (game-ending) if still alive at end of game, or if only player alive
○ Non-targeting ability, meaning this ability counts as either being used or not and
does not show up when being tracked or watched as well as the action being
unaffected by switch
○ Immune to control

**Action Priority**
Roles’ actions resolve in the following order:
**Action Role**

1. On guard Veteran
2. Switch Bus Driver, Limo Driver (resolves in that order)
3. Control Witch
4. Seduce Escort / Hooker
5. Frame Framer
6. Invest./Scrutin. Cop / Detective
7. Protect/Defend Doctor / Bodyguard / Survivor
8. (All killing actions) Vigilante / Bomb / Godfather / Sniper / Jester / Mass Murderer / Serial Killer / Arsonist
9. Corrupt Yakuza
10. Douse Arsonist
11. Dispose Janitor
12. Follow/Watch Tracker or Stalker / Watcher or Lookout
13. Sabotage Saboteur
14. Gain memory Amnesiac
15. All deaths happen at the end (actions still go through even if you die)
